About VisuRef

VisuRef is a powerful web-based tool designed specifically for artists, illustrators, and painters who need to analyze and manipulate reference images. Whether you're studying composition, analyzing color palettes, or preparing reference materials for your artwork, VisuRef provides the tools you need to enhance your creative process.

Key Features

  • Image Filters: Apply various filters like grayscale and brightness adjustments to analyze different aspects of your reference images
  • Real-time Processing: See changes instantly as you adjust parameters
  • Modular Design: Enable or disable different effects to focus on specific aspects of your reference
  • Easy Upload: Simply drag and drop or select images from your device
  • Responsive Interface: Works seamlessly on desktop and tablet devices
  • No Installation Required: Runs entirely in your web browser

VisuRef is perfect for artists who want to:

  • Study the tonal values in reference images by converting them to grayscale
  • Analyze composition and lighting by adjusting brightness and contrast
  • Prepare reference materials for traditional or digital painting
  • Understand color relationships and value structures
  • Create study materials for art classes or workshops

The tool is designed with simplicity in mind, allowing you to focus on your artistic analysis rather than complex software interfaces. Whether you're a beginner artist learning the fundamentals or an experienced professional refining your technique, VisuRef provides the tools you need to enhance your reference image analysis workflow.
