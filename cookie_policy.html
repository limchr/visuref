What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit, preferences, and device.

Types of cookies used on this website

  • Essential cookies: Required for website operation (e.g. session cookies). These do not require consent.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to improve the website (Google Analytics). Only set with your explicit consent.
  • External services: Services like PayPal or Patreon may set cookies when you click their buttons. These are subject to their own privacy policies.

Consent & withdrawal

Before non-essential cookies are placed, you will be asked for your explicit consent (via the cookie banner). You can withdraw consent or adjust your preferences at any time, e.g. through a “Cookie settings” link at the bottom of the site.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings (block, delete, or restrict). Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality. We also recommend using the cookie banner controls for granular choices.

Google Analytics (only with consent)

If you consent, Google Analytics (with IP anonymization enabled) is used to analyze site usage. Data is processed to improve the website. You may withdraw consent at any time.

Developer implementation note

Non-essential scripts (e.g. Google Analytics) must not load until consent has been given. Example (pseudo-code):

    // load GA script here
  }
</script>
